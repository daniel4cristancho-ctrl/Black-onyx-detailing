Our Standard

Water-based products.
A different level.

"We do not just clean cars. We restore them to the standard they were built for."

We exclusively use water-based dressings on all black trim, tires, and plastics. Oil-based products attract dust and leave a greasy residue within days. Ours dry to the touch.

Your vehicle stays cleaner longer — with a natural, factory-fresh appearance that actively repels dust, dirt, and moisture. That is the Black Onyx difference.
